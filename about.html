Mapping environmental change from space.

Hello! I'm Sara Aharon, a Geography student at Alexandru Ioan Cuza University of Iași, Romania, specializing in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), remote sensing, and environmental monitoring.

My work focuses on using satellite imagery and cloud-based geospatial technologies to understand how landscapes change over time. I enjoy combining Google Earth Engine, ArcGIS, Landsat, Sentinel-2, and web mapping technologies to transform complex spatial data into meaningful environmental insights.

My long-term goal is to contribute to international Earth Observation projects that support sustainable environmental management and climate resilience.
