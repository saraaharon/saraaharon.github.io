About Me

I am Sara Aharon, a Geography student specializing in Geographic Information Systems, remote sensing and Earth Observation.

My work focuses on applying satellite imagery and geospatial technologies to understand environmental change.

My research interests include satellite-based monitoring, coastal dynamics, environmental change detection and the use of cloud-based platforms such as Google Earth Engine.
